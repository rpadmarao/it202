About stark industries

Stark Industries is now a technology and clean energy company

Our most important technology, currently, is the ARC Reactor, a sustainable reactor powered by a core of palladium. We have a working model that powers Stark Tower, as well as another in our California headquarters.

The most recent project that we have tackled for a third party, the U.S. Military, is one we are quite proud of. We designed and manufactured the repulsors in use on the S.H.I.E.L.D. helicarriers. They are large scale replicas of the repulsors used as flight stabilizers on the Iron Man suit.
